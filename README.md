### Selected work

**[Motion Playground](https://pengxu-work.pages.dev/work/motion-playground)** — A 12-chapter SwiftUI motion reference with 70+ runnable `#Preview` blocks. iOS 17 → 26: Animatable, KeyframeAnimator, PhaseAnimator, Metal shaders, Liquid Glass. Generated by 12 parallel research sub-agents, one per chapter.

**[Genable](https://pengxu-work.pages.dev/work/genable)** — Quality-first AI design generator inside Figma. Full agentic loop: intent → generation → feedback → self-correction. Multi-model orchestration for layout, palette, and copy.

**[Hermes](https://pengxu-work.pages.dev/work/hermes)** — Local-first agent runtime. Tools, memory, and taste are all yours to edit. Not a black-box terminal — a readable, intervenable process dialogue.

**[Field Notes](https://pengxu-work.pages.dev/work/field-notes)** — Living design system for AI-native interfaces. From tokens and components to the full vocabulary of generative states: empty, loading, error, out-of-distribution.

**[Stage](https://pengxu-work.pages.dev/work/stage)** — Two seasons documenting Shenzhen City Ballet — annual performances and masterclass nights. Shared palette and framing across multi-campus shoots.
