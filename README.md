### Who I am

I am Peng Xu, a designer who moved deeper into engineering because AI products need sharper interface judgment, faster prototyping, and more direct ownership of the shipped experience.

My background is visual design, product interface work, photography, and design systems. My current work sits where Figma, TypeScript, agents, and product taste meet.

### What I am building

- AI-native interface tools that turn intent into editable, inspectable product surfaces.
- Agent-facing design infrastructure: Figma plugins, MCP servers, typed UI operations, and verification loops.
- Consumer AI products where the model can act on the interface instead of only answering inside a chat box.
